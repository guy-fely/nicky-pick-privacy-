Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

This privacy policy describes how Nicky Pick ("we", "us", "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

Account Data

We use anonymous authentication — no email, name, phone number, or social login is required. Your account is identified only by a randomly generated unique ID (UUID).

Gameplay Data

We store the following on our servers to enable game features:

  • In-game currency balances (hearts)
  • Level progress and unlock status
  • Run history (scores, collectibles, completion status)
  • Purchased upgrades and cosmetics
  • Daily reward streak

Device & Local Data

The app stores preferences, achievements, and cached game state locally on your device using secure storage. This data is never transmitted to our servers.

Advertising Data

We use Google AdMob to show ads. The AdMob SDK may collect:

  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA on iOS, Advertising ID on Android)
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Approximate location (network-based, not GPS)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

This data is collected by Google under Google's Privacy Policy.

Security & Anti-Abuse Data

To protect fair gameplay, our servers log:

  • IP addresses associated with security events (e.g., account resets, rate limit violations)
  • Timestamps and event types for audit purposes

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Gameplay: Save your progress, track scores, manage in-game economy
  • Ads: Display relevant advertisements via Google AdMob
  • Security: Detect cheating, abuse, and unauthorized access
  • Improvements: Understand aggregate gameplay patterns (no individual profiling)

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data is shared only with:

  • Supabase — our backend provider (Supabase Privacy Policy)
  • Google AdMob — ad serving (Google Privacy Policy)

4. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Nicky Pick does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app uses anonymous authentication — no personally identifiable information is collected from any user. Ad content is configured for general audiences.

If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

5. Data Retention

  • Gameplay data is retained as long as your account exists.
  • Security logs (IP addresses, audit events) are retained for abuse prevention.
  • Ad-related data is managed by Google per their retention policies.

6. Your Rights & Data Deletion

Nicky Picky (developed by Bubblegum Incorporated) allows you to request deletion of your data in two ways:

Option A: In-App Reset

Open the app → Settings → Reset Account. This immediately deletes:

  • All gameplay progress and run history
  • In-game currency balances
  • Purchased upgrades and cosmetics
  • Level unlock status

Option B: Full Account Deletion via Email

To request complete deletion of all your data, follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to bubblegum0incorporated@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: "Data Deletion Request"
  3. Include your in-app User ID (found in Settings) if possible

We will process your request within 30 days. The following data will be permanently deleted:

  • Anonymous authentication record
  • All gameplay data (progress, scores, currency, upgrades, cosmetics)
  • Daily reward and challenge history

The following data may be retained for up to 90 days after deletion for security and abuse prevention:

  • Security audit logs (IP addresses, timestamps of flagged events)

Ad-related data collected by Google AdMob is managed by Google. You can manage your ad preferences at Google's Data & Privacy page.

7. Security

We use encryption for authentication tokens (device secure storage), server-side validation for all transactions, rate limiting, and audit logging to protect your data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here with an updated date. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
